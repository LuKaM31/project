What diseases does tobacco cause?
Tobacco causes: diseases of the respiratory tract, diseases of the stomach and duodenum, the penetration of carbon dioxide into the blood, cigarettes contribute to the development of tumors, various diseases of the lungs and heart, an increase in blood pressure... however. , that's not all. Cigarettes cause many more terrible diseases that would take us too far to list.
